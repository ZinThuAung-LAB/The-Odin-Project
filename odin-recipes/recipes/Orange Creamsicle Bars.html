Orange Creamsicle Bars

Description

These orange creamsicle bars are the creamy, dreamy, easy, no-bake treat you've been waiting for! Don't be fooled by the number of steps—these bars are not difficult to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups finely ground graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 (0.25 ounce) packet unflavored powdered gelatin/li>
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice, divided
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest

Steps

  1. Line a 9x9-inch square pan with enough parchment paper to have overhang on all sides.
  2. In a large bowl, beat heavy cream, powdered sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract with an electric mixer until mixture holds stiff peaks; refrigerate until needed.
  3. Place gelatin into a small microwave-safe bowl. Pour 1/4 cup orange juice over gelatin and allow it to bloom for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove whipped cream from the refrigerator. Pour cream cheese mixture over whipped cream and fold until thoroughly combined.
  5. To the remaining filling, add orange zest, lemon zest, and orange food coloring. Fold together until evenly combined.
